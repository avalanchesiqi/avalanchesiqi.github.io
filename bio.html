I will join the CSMR Lab at the University of Michigan as a research fellow in 2021. Currently, I am a research fellow in the Computational Media Lab at the Australian National University. I obtained my Ph.D. (Computer Science) from the Australian National University, M.S. (Information Technology) from the University of Melbourne, and B.E. (Electronics Engineering) from Tianjin University. My Ph.D. advisors are Prof. Lexing Xie and Dr. Marian-Andrei Rizoiu.

I am broadly interested in measuring and modeling collective social behavior on the web and social media. Some previous work includes how YouTube recommender systems drive user attention (CSCW'19, WSDM'21), why some YouTube videos are more engaging (ICWSM'18), and how Twitter sampling impacts common measurements (ICWSM'20). My work lies in the fields of computational social science and social computing.
